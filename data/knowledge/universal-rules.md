# Universal Rules — All Roof Systems

These rules apply to EVERY estimate regardless of roof system.

## Underlayment
- **OC Titanium PSU 30** ($145/roll, covers 2 SQ) — ALWAYS required on every roof. No exceptions.
  - This is the PRIMARY underlayment. Do NOT substitute Grace Ice & Water High Temp.
  - Grace Ice & Water is a supplemental product for specific areas (valleys, eaves on ice-prone roofs), NOT a replacement for PSU 30.

## Equipment & Fees (Always Include)
- **Porto Potty** — 1 per job. Covers delivery ($150) + pickup ($150) + monthly rental.
- **Landfill/Dumpster** — 1 per job minimum. This is a landfill disposal fee, not a dumpster rental.
  - If tear-off: quantity = CEILING(total_squares / 15) rolloffs
- **Fuel Charge** ($194) — 1 per job
- **Aspen Reprographic** ($55/ea) — For permit drawings. Include if permits mentioned.

## Overnights
- $387/night
- Auto-include when **Sergio** or **Hugo** is the selected labor crew
- Also suggest when job is >25 SQ (large job likely multi-day)

## Labor Rules
- Only ONE crew per job (Hugo, Alfredo, Chris, Sergio — these are alternatives, not additions)
- **Hugo** has three rate tiers based on predominant pitch:
  - Hugo (12/12 pitch): $750/sq — for 12/12 pitch roofs
  - Hugo (lower pitch): $650/sq — for 8/12 to 11/12 pitch
  - Hugo (standard): $550/sq — for pitches below 8/12
- **Alfredo**: $1,136/sq
- **Chris**: $750/sq
- **Sergio**: $129/sq
- Labor unit is per square (1 SQ = 100 SF)

## Tear-Off Rules
- When tear-off is part of the job:
  - Include **Rolloff** at $48/sq — quantity = CEILING(total_squares / 15)
  - Include **7/16" OSB** at $24.35/sheet — quantity = total_squares × 3
  - Include **2X4 Toe Boards** for OSHA safety

## Calculated Accessories

### Heat Tape (All Systems)
Formula:
- triangles = CEILING(eave_length / 3)
- eave_cable = triangles × 6 LF (3' up + 3' down per triangle)
- valley_cable = valley_length (full straight run)
- total_LF = eave_cable + valley_cable
- Material: Heat Tape at $5.00/LF
- Labor: Heat Tape Install at $7.50/LF

### Skylights (Optional)
- $2,400 each — manual count entry
- NOT included in estimate total — shown as "Optional Items"
- If Velux specified: Velux FCM4646 ($459) + Flash Kit ECL4646 ($129) per unit

## Financial Rules
- Sales tax: 10% on materials ONLY (not labor or equipment)
- Margin, waste %, office overhead %, sundries % — set per estimate by user
- Vendor quote items are READ-ONLY (quantities and prices from quote are source of truth)

## Quantity Calculation (from RoofScope)
- 1 square (SQ) = 100 square feet
- Starter quantity: (eave_length + rake_length) / coverage
- Valley quantity: valley_length / coverage
- Eave/Drip quantity: eave_length / coverage
- Rake quantity: rake_length / coverage
- Ridge/H&R quantity: (ridge_length + hip_length) / coverage
- Flat fee items (delivery, fuel, porto, rolloff): quantity = 1 unless specified

## Common Accessories (All Systems)
- **4in1 Pipe Jack** ($16/ea) — for 1.5" to 3" pipe penetrations
- **4" Boot Galv** ($20/ea) — for 4" pipe penetrations
- **Split Boot** ($40/ea) — for repairs around existing penetrations
- **Airhawk RVG 50** ($34/ea) — roof vent, 50 sq in net free area
- **Small Broan 636** ($34/ea) — 3-4" round duct exhaust cap
- **Large Broan 634** ($49/ea) — 6" round duct exhaust cap
- **Rolled Ridge Vent** ($69/roll) — continuous attic ventilation
- Sealants and caulk are covered by sundries % — do NOT select unless explicitly requested

## What NOT to Auto-Select
- Do NOT select sealants, caulk, spray paint, nails, or screws unless:
  - Explicitly mentioned in job description with a quantity
  - Part of a vendor quote (vendor items always selected)
- These are covered by the Sundries/Misc Materials percentage
